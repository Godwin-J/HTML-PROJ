A paragraph is a group of sentences that develops a single idea. It is a subdivision of a written work that can be a short composition or note. Paragraphs are the building blocks of papers. Characteristics of a paragraph A paragraph usually begins on a new line, which is often indented. A paragraph can be a narrative, descriptive, or other type of paragraph. A paragraph should have a topic sentence that indicates the main idea of the paragraph. A paragraph should have sentences that support the main idea. A paragraph should maintain a consistent flow.
